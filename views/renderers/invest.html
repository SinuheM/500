500 is pleased to offer accredited U.S. investors* the opportunity to invest in one or more of our funds. We are currently accepting a limited set of investors to the following funds:

  • Fund III - global flagship fund
  • Fund I Annex - follow-on fund for our first generation global flagship fund (i.e., Fund I)
  • 500 Luchadores - regional fund focusing on Latin America
  • 500 Durians - regional fund focusing on Southeast Asia

To learn more about our fund offerings, please provide us with some information about yourself and your interests. Our investor relations team will review your application and may contact you for further information. If you are selected, you will receive access to our investor presentation and related fund materials via SeedInvest, our third-party platform partner. You will also have direct access to the 500 Startups team to answer any questions you may have about the fund(s) and/or our organization in general.

If, after reviewing our fund materials, you decide to pursue an investment in one of our funds, you will be able to submit a request for admission and walk through the accreditation verification process on the SeedInvest platform, which will be focused on either your annual income or net worth (for individuals). As an individual, you can qualify as an accredited investor by either (a) having more than $200,000 in annual income for each of the past two years (or $300,000 with your spouse) and a reasonable expectation of earning the same this year or (b) having a net worth of at least $1 million (not including your principal residence).
